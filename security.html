Security & Privacy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Our Commitment

Fracttalix takes the security and privacy of your data seriously. This page explains our practices in plain language.

Our Products

  • Sentinel — streaming anomaly detection with 4-layer detector
  • VeriScript / VeriScore — deterministic scientific claim scoring SaaS

Data We Collect

  • Only the minimum data required for the product to function (time-series values for Sentinel; document text for VeriScore)
  • No training on customer data
  • No third-party analytics or tracking scripts unless explicitly consented

Security Practices

  • All data in transit is encrypted (TLS 1.3)
  • All stored data is encrypted at rest
  • Access is limited to the minimum necessary (principle of least privilege)
  • We maintain audit logs of system access
  • Regular vulnerability scans and code reviews
  • Automated testing (760+ passing tests) on every release

Data Retention & Deletion

Customer data is retained only as long as needed for the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time via email support. We will delete it within 30 days.

Compliance Posture

We follow industry-standard practices for a small SaaS company. We are preparing for formal SOC 2 Type 1 certification once paid product usage reaches scale. We comply with applicable data protection laws (including GDPR where relevant).

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting customer data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe for payments (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Secure, SOC 2-compliant cloud infrastructure for hosting
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing
